Motor and Engineering Company of Ethiopia (Moenco)

APPRENTICE/BODY AND PAINT CATEGORY
 The Motor and Engineering Company of Ethiopia (Moenco) has an apprenticeship program for recent graduates of 2020 and 2021 with good academic achievement in the field of
Auto Mechanics,
Auto Electricity (Level III, Level V or Advanced Diploma) for Apprentice Program. Therefore, interested applicants can apply in web address indicated below
JOB REQUIREMENT
Required Qualification and Criteria: College Diploma (Level III, Level V, Level VI or Advanced Diploma) in General Mechanics, General Metal Fabrication or in related fields

Deadline Date : August 1, 2021
HOW TO APPLY
 All applicants should submit their document with COC certification
Only graduates of recent year will be considered in the program
Female applicants are highly encouraged to apply
Place of work: Addis Ababa, Adama Branch, Awassa Branch, Bahirdar Branch and Diredawa Branch offices Applicants who meet the above requirements can submit application on website: https://moencoethiopia.com/job-opportunities
from 12/07/2021 to 31/07/2021
The Motor and Engineering Company of Ethiopia (Moenco) is an equal opportunity employer and is looking for passionate, capable and competent
candidates to its team who sustain Company purpose.
